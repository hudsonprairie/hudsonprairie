Careers at HudsonPrairie

We are building a modern, people-first staffing and consulting firm. If you enjoy working closely with clients and candidates, and like clear, honest communication, HudsonPrairie could be a great place for you.

Why Work With Us

Hands-on Impact

Work directly with hiring managers, candidates and partners. See your work make a visible difference.

Growth Mindset

Plenty of room to learn new skills across recruiting, client management, operations and technology.

Flexible & Collaborative

We value flexibility, clear expectations and a supportive environment over unnecessary complexity.

Current Opportunities

As a growing firm, our internal openings will change over time. Below are example roles that may be part of our team. You can also send a general application.

Recruiter / Talent Partner

Work with candidates and hiring managers across IT and professional roles. Experience in staffing or recruiting is helpful.

Account Manager

Build and maintain relationships with client teams, understand their hiring needs and coordinate with delivery.

Operations / Coordination

Support onboarding, compliance, timesheets and day-to-day operations that keep everything running smoothly.

Send a General Application

If you’re interested in future opportunities with HudsonPrairie, you can share your details and we’ll keep you in mind as we grow.
