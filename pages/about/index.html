About HudsonPrairie Solutions

HudsonPrairie Solutions is a modern staffing and consulting partner focused on delivering high-quality talent and practical technology solutions. We connect organizations with professionals across Information Technology, Healthcare, Finance & Accounting, Engineering, and more.

Our approach is simple: listen carefully, move quickly, and never compromise on quality. Whether you are scaling a team, filling a critical role, or looking for your next opportunity, we aim to make every interaction clear, transparent, and human.

What We Stand For

  • Reliability: We follow through on what we promise.
  • Clarity: No jargon, no confusion – just honest communication.
  • Partnership: We treat every client and candidate relationship as long-term.
  • Quality: We focus on fit, not just filling seats.

Who We Serve

We support companies ranging from growing startups to established enterprises, as well as professionals at different stages of their careers – from mid-level specialists to seasoned experts.
