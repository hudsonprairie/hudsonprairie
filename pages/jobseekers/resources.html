Simple Checklists

  • ✅ Resume has clear summary, tech stack and 3–5 key projects.
  • ✅ LinkedIn headline mentions your role and key skills.
  • ✅ You have 2–3 strong stories for “challenge / action / result”.
  • ✅ You know your preferred locations, salary range and role types.
