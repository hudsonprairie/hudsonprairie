Consulting & Project Support

Beyond staffing individual roles, HudsonPrairie can support focused initiatives and projects with small, specialized teams and advisory support.

Where We Can Help

Technology & Cloud

Support migrations, modernizations and integrations around cloud, data and applications.

Identity & Access

Practical help around IAM, Entra ID / Azure AD, access reviews and security-focused initiatives.

Process & Delivery

Improve ways of working, documentation, workflows and handoffs across teams.

Engagement Models

  1. Advisory support – part-time guidance, reviews and recommendations.
  2. Small delivery pods – a lean group of specialists focused on clear outcomes.
  3. Hybrid model – combine consulting with contract staffing to fill gaps.

How We Start

  • Initial discovery call to clarify goals and constraints.
  • Outline of scope, timing and recommended resourcing.
  • Agreement on deliverables and communication cadence.
