Contract-to-Hire

Contract-to-hire gives both you and the professional time to validate day-to-day fit before committing to a permanent hire.

Why Choose Contract-to-Hire?

Reduce Hiring Risk

See real work, collaboration style and performance before making a long-term decision.

Faster Starts

Get someone in-seat and productive while you finalize approvals or budget.

Better Mutual Fit

Both sides can evaluate the role, team and expectations over time.

Typical Contract-to-Hire Flow

  1. Define the role – responsibilities, conversion plan and expected timeline.
  2. Engage on contract – the professional starts as a contractor with clear expectations.
  3. Evaluate performance – ongoing feedback from both the manager and the contractor.
  4. Decide on conversion – if it’s a match, we coordinate the transition to full-time.
