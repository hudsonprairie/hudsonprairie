Staffing Solutions

HudsonPrairie provides flexible staffing models to help you scale teams, fill critical gaps and deliver projects on time — without over-complicating the process.

Flexible Ways to Engage Talent

Contract / Temporary

Short-term or project-based professionals to handle peak workloads, specialist needs or backfills.

Contract-to-Hire

Evaluate fit on the job before making a long-term hire, reducing risk for both the team and the candidate.

Permanent Hiring

Full-time talent sourced, screened and supported through the offer and onboarding process.

Our Approach

  1. Understand the role & context – team, stack, initiatives, culture and expectations.
  2. Search & screening – active sourcing, targeted outreach and structured conversations.
  3. Shortlist & feedback – a small, focused set of well-matched profiles.
  4. Interview coordination – scheduling, prep and follow-up with both sides.
  5. Offer & onboarding support – ensuring a smooth start and clear communication.

Roles We Commonly Support

Software & Cloud Engineers Data & Analytics Identity & Security Project & Product Business Analysts Support & Operations

Need something specific?

Even if your requirement doesn’t fit neatly into a standard role title, we’re happy to discuss it and suggest a practical way forward.
